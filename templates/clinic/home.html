Why this platform exists

Teachers are facing a growing mental health crisis driven by workload pressure, financial stress, stigma, and limited access to counseling. KEWOTA supports structured, confidential access to care.

  • • Guided feedback after sessions to improve care quality
  • • M-PESA payments (paybill/till and STK push)
  • • Separate workflows for reception, clinicians, and clients

Key highlights (Kenya)

• Reports shared by KEWOTA indicate a worrying rise in teacher suicide cases since 2022.

• Depression, anxiety, and substance abuse are recurring drivers reported by educators and stakeholders.

• Nationally, suicide and limited access to mental health services remain major public health concerns.

This content is for awareness and advocacy; clinical support is delivered by licensed professionals.
